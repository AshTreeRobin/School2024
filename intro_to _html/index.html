What and Who are Bishops of the Old Faith

Leshy

The youngest of the five siblings, a demonic, grassy worm who became the god of choas through the green crown. After the attack by his brother Narinder, he is left with bandages covering where his eye used, he is still able to see through the power of the green crown. He also became the represtation of see no evil after this fight. He rules over Darkwoods, a lush, grassy land with healing Camelias - Which he is shown to be fond of. Though being the youngest, he is not overly dramatic or emotional and willingly is commanded under his elder siblings.

Hekt

The second youngest, the bearer of the yellow crown, is a four eyed frog, the god of famine. After her brother cut at her throat she uses bandages to hold her throat closed, thus is speak no evil. Her land is Anura, a golden, almost fall like land filled with many frogs like creatures and mushrooms. She acts confident, bleeding to overconfidence with her power taking even a more dominant role then her brother Kallamar.

Kallamar

Second eldest, he is a squid possessing the blue crown. His ears have ben damanged in the fight against his younger brother and thus is bandaged. God of Pestilence he also, after his injury, represents hear no evil. He rules Anchordeep; a sea like biome with fish and sunken ships. A known coward, he is more under his siblings - including the two younger. Quick to run, placate, and beg.

Shamura

The eldest sibling, God of War, and bearer of the purple crown. They are a spider, whos bandaged head covers their exposed brain, which was caused by their sibling Narinder. Due to their brains exposure their crown burrows into their head with tentical like veins. Their realm is Silks Cradle, a land with spiders not wholy unlike themselves. They are the most duity bound of their siblings, with great intelligence.

The One Who Waits - Narinder

A demonic cat who was the third oldest of his siblings, who found the red crown, turned into the god of death. He rose to power with his siblings helping defeat the old gods in the times before. After having ideas of change - dispite being the only sibling over a constant force - from his eldest sibling Shamura, the god fought his siblings. He lost, not before wounding his siblings, and was punished by being sealed in his own realm of death in chains with his two disciples. Where he seeks revenge against them using what little power he has to send vessels with the red crown to defeat them and free him, none of succeeded; however, one fateful day a lamb is sent to his realm, the last one, who may turn the tides.
