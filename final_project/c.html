Olive Specter

She is a strange one, living surrounded by graves, some of her husbands who died tragically one after the other. In her younger years, she tracked down the Grim Reaper for answers for her lovers' misfortune. However, she fell in love with the reaper and had a passionate night with him. She seemingly fell pregnant with a child soon after however, the child was taken away. The number of graves in her yard seemed to grow as she seeks out her undead lover. And nothing will stop her from seeing her love.
